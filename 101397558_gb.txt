1. Location: George Brown College (GBC) is located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
   It has multiple campuses throughout the city, including the St. James Campus,
   Waterfront Campus, and Casa Loma Campus.
2. Diverse Student Body: GBC is known for its diverse student population, with students 
   from over 100 countries. This diversity creates a rich and multicultural learning environment.
3. Programs and Courses: The college offers a wide range of programs and courses in various fields,
   including business, health sciences, hospitality, technology, and the arts. It provides both
   diploma and degree programs to meet the educational needs of its students.
